Come Look at Brandon's Arsenal

Motiv Tag

The Motiv Tag is meant for short to medium oil, and is stronger than a typical light-oil ball. I picked up this ball just recently after a lot of hestitation, but I love it!

⊗

DV8 Vandal

The DV8 Vandal is meant for medium to long oil. I really only bring out this ball during tournaments since it's way too strong for normal daily play.

⊗

Storm Punch-Out

The Storm Punch-Out is meant for short to medium oil, and is meant to really snap in the back-end. This is one of my go-to balls for normal everyday play and especially for tournaments.

⊗

Hammer Bad Intentions

The Hammer Bad Intentions is an extremely strong ball that has a smooth motion throughout. Like the Vandal and Marvel S, I only bring this ball out during tournaments because of its overwhelming strength.

⊗

Storm Marvel S

The Storm Marvel-S is a recreation of the Storm Marvel, but with a solid coverstock that enables it to have a smooth motion and finish strong in the back-end. Like the Vandal and the Bad Intentions, I only bring this ball out during tournaments since it is extremely strong. It is the strongest of all six balls that I own.

⊗

Ebonite Gamebreaker

The Ebonite GameBreaker 2 is a great go-to ball for a lot of bowlers, because of its controllable motion on the lane and its hard hitting power. I bring this out occasionally if I want something that hits really strong and is still controllable.
